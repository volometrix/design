Definitions


Accounts Contacted
Accounts to whom Symantec/Region sent an email or with whom they met, but collaboration wasn't substantiative enough to qualify as “engaged”

Accounts Engaged
Accounts with whom Symantec/Region had at least one (1) meeting and spent at least 30 minutes during the period.

Cumulative Engaged
Running total of Accounts with whom Symantec/Region has engaged since April 15.
